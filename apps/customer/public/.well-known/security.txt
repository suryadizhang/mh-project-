# Security Policy

We take security seriously and appreciate responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities.

## Reporting Vulnerabilities

If you discover a security vulnerability, please:

1. Email us at security@myhibachi.com
2. Provide detailed information about the vulnerability
3. Allow us reasonable time to respond and fix the issue
4. Do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until it has been resolved

## Response Timeline

- Initial response: Within 24 hours
- Status update: Within 72 hours  
- Resolution target: Within 30 days for critical issues

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- myhibachi.com and all subdomains
- Mobile applications
- API endpoints
- Backend services

## Out of Scope

- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks
- Denial of Service (DoS) attacks
- Spam or phishing attacks

Thank you for helping keep My Hibachi secure!
